Process

How the Foundation Works

A rigid, multi-phase lifecycle ensures that every line of the specification is vetted by the community before official release.

1

Exploratory Research

Active Working Group research into emerging AI primitives.

2

RFC & Proposal (AEP)

Public request for comments and formal enhancement proposal submission.

3

Implementation & Conformance

Proving the spec through reference code and validation tools.

4

Formal Release

Final ratification and inclusion in the Foundation registry.
